My top 3 movies of all times

The Notebook

I love this movie because Ryan Gosling looks amazing here and so does the love story it has to it.

The Beauty and the Beast (Disney Version)

So this classic is simply wonderful. I love how this highly intelligent woman is able to see past the facade of this monster and fall in love with him completely because of his personality and heart. Even though things started off a little shady, these two worked it out and that's really cute.

Léon: The Professional

First of all; The acting of Natalie Portman here was impressive. It has a very different plot and the way the ultimate killer develops such caring for this little girl to the point that he would give away his own life to save her... I have to admit it made me cry a lot .
